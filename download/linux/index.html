Surge Engine for Linux

Surge Engine is an open-source engine for retro games. It has brought joy to many people, including you! Help to expand this work and to inspire more people! Become a supporter!

Here's a sample of what you get with the engine:

  • Play Surge the Rabbit on your Linux PC!
  • Create your own games and share them with your friends!
  • Get a safe and trusted open-source software that respects you.
  • Learn gamedev and get entertained – possibly for years!
  • Play freely, with no ads and no microtransactions!

I'm Alexandre, the developer of the engine. I've been making this engine freely available over the years – for your joy. However, writing open-source has been a difficult journey. I'm now asking for support to help maintain it. Thank you to all the generous fans who are supporting this work!

Donations are secure and powered by Ko-fi. A big thank you if you increase the amount!
